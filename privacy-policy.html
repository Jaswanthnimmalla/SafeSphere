Privacy-First Approach: SafeSphere is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All your data is stored locally on your device with military-grade encryption. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

1. Information Collection and Use

SafeSphere operates completely offline and does not collect any personal information from users.

1.1 Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored ONLY on your device:

  • Passwords: Encrypted with AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Private Files: Stored in encrypted Privacy Vault
  • Security Settings: Your app preferences and configurations
  • Biometric Data: Handled by Android's secure hardware, never accessed by SafeSphere

1.2 Data NOT Collected

SafeSphere does NOT collect:

  • ‚ùå Personal identification information
  • ‚ùå Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ‚ùå Location data
  • ‚ùå Usage statistics or analytics
  • ‚ùå Crash reports or diagnostics
  • ‚ùå Any data transmitted to external servers

2. Data Security

Your security is our priority:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM (Advanced Encryption Standard)
  • Local Storage: Data never leaves your device
  • Biometric Protection: Uses Android's hardware-backed Keystore for biometric authentication
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data stays on your device, period
  • No Third-Party Services: We don't use any analytics, crash reporting, or advertising services

3. AutofillService

SafeSphere uses Android's AutofillService API to provide password autofill functionality:

  • AutofillService only accesses login forms when you actively tap on them
  • Credentials are provided directly from your encrypted local vault
  • No data is transmitted to SafeSphere servers (we don't have any!)
  • You have full control and can disable AutofillService anytime in Android Settings

4. Permissions

SafeSphere requests the following permissions:

  • BIND_AUTOFILL_SERVICE: To provide password autofill functionality
  • USE_BIOMETRIC: For fingerprint/face unlock (data handled by Android, not SafeSphere)
  • CAMERA: Optional, only if you choose to scan QR codes or documents
  • READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Optional, only if you choose to import/export files to Privacy Vault

All permissions are used solely for their stated purpose and can be revoked anytime.

5. Third-Party Services

SafeSphere does NOT use any third-party services.

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No cloud storage providers
  • No social media integrations

6. Children's Privacy

SafeSphere does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app can be safely used by all age groups.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All data is stored ONLY on your device
  • You can delete individual items anytime within the app
  • Uninstalling SafeSphere permanently deletes all data
  • We have no backups or copies (because we never had access to your data!)

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
